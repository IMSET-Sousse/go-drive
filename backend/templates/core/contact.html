Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and a form of identification. International customers may need additional documentation.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours before the scheduled pickup time are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 48 hours in advance may be subject to a fee.

Do you offer insurance options?

Yes, we offer various insurance options including basic coverage, comprehensive coverage, and premium protection packages. Details can be discussed at the time of booking.
